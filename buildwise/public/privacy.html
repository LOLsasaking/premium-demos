Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026 · Early-access beta

The short version

BuildWise AI is designed so that most of your data never leaves your device. We collect as little as possible, we don't sell any of it, and you can ask us to delete what we have at any time.

What we collect

  • Waitlist email. If you join the early-access list, we store the email address you give us and use it only to contact you about your access. Nothing else.
  • Your projects. Interview answers and generated construction packages are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. They are not uploaded to our servers in the default experience.
  • Uploads and AI processing. If (and only if) a live AI backend is enabled, the interview answers and images you upload are sent to our server and processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate your plans. They are used to produce your results, not to build advertising profiles.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell or rent your personal information.
  • We don't use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We don't email you for anything other than early access unless you opt in.

Data retention & deletion

Projects live in your browser — clearing your browser data removes them. To remove your email from the waitlist or ask about anything held on our side, contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

Changes

If this policy changes materially during the beta, we'll note it on this page with a new date.

Questions or deletion requests: reach out through the contact option on the main page and we'll respond promptly.
